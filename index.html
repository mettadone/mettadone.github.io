About Mettadone

The project is based drummer and author of music Eugene Shamarin in March 2014. After some time vocalist Maxim Ilyashenko joins to group. In the period from January to September 2015 studio NC records was recorded debut album, titled "Invisible Disease". At the moment, the band signed a contract with Ukrainian label WOLFSHADE RECORDS, and is preparing to release an album, scheduled for December 7, 2015. After the release is planned the whole set of commands for appearances.
